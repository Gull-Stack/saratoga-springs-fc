Youth Academy

Our Youth Academy is the foundation of the SSFC player pathway. Designed for the youngest players, this program introduces children to soccer through fun, game-based activities that develop fundamental motor skills, coordination, and a love for the sport.

Led by Head Coach Nick Christensen, the academy focuses on creating a positive, encouraging environment where every child feels welcome and excited to play.

  • Small-sided games and age-appropriate activities
  • Focus on fundamental movement and ball skills
  • Introduction to teamwork and sportsmanship
  • Low player-to-coach ratio (8:1 max)
  • Weekly sessions with optional match days
  • All equipment provided for first-timers

Competitive Teams

Our competitive program is for players ready to take their game to the next level. Teams compete in established leagues and tournaments throughout Utah and the region.

Each age group has dedicated coaching staff who create tailored training plans focusing on technical skill development, tactical understanding, physical conditioning, and mental resilience.

  • Boys and girls teams from U8 through U18
  • League play in Utah Youth Soccer Association
  • Regional and national tournament participation
  • 3 training sessions per week + match weekends
  • USSF-licensed coaching staff
  • Player evaluations and development tracking
  • College preparation guidance for U16-U18
