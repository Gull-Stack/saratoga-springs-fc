Our Story

Saratoga Springs Football Club was founded in 2021 with a clear mission: to create a youth soccer club that puts player development and community at its core.

What started as a small group of families who shared a passion for soccer has grown into one of the most exciting youth clubs in Utah County. Head Coach Nick Christensen saw a need for quality, accessible soccer training in Saratoga Springs â€” and SSFC was born.

Today, we serve over 200 players across more than 20 teams, offering programs from introductory Youth Academy sessions for 4-year-olds to competitive travel teams for high schoolers.

Mission & Values

Our vision is simple but powerful: "To form a community of competitive soccer families together."

We believe that youth soccer is about more than wins and losses. It's about building character, fostering teamwork, developing athletic ability, and creating lifelong friendships.
