Player Registration

Join the Saratoga Springs FC family. Follow the steps below to register your player.

How to Register

Getting your player registered is easy. Follow these four simple steps.

1

Choose a Program

Review our programs and select the one that's right for your player's age and skill level.

2

Fill Out the Form

Complete the registration form below with your player's information and parent/guardian details.

3

Submit Payment

Pay the registration fee online. Sibling discounts and payment plans are available.

4

Get Ready to Play!

You'll receive a confirmation email with team assignment, schedule, and what to bring to the first session.
